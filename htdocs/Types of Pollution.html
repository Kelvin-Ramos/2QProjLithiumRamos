Types of Pollution
Land Pollution
Land pollution is a type of pollution, in which land or a solid area is polluted. This is caused by human activities. This type of pollution is composed of plastics, styrofoams, clothes, glasses, and paper. Land pollution is mainly caused by everyday garbage, mining, farming, industries, and factories. This pollution mainly causes flood and endangers one's health. Lastly, it can degrade the quality of the soil, which affects the plants and crops planted in it.
Water Pollution
Water pollution is another kind of pollution, in which pollutants are found in any body of water. This can also be caused by the materials we use everyday that are thrown or that got moved to the bodies of water. This greatly impact the life in that water like fish, corals, and plants, and it deprives people of clean source of water. It can be because of gasoline leaks, trashes, or algae blooms.
Air Pollution
Air pollution is the third kind of pollution. Air pollution is the production of any pollutant to the air. This is the most common kind of pollution. It can be due to smoke-producing vehicles, factories, fire, etc. This can affect not only the weather but also our health. It can make climate change even worse. Also, air pollutants can make adverse effects to our lungs and our pulmonary system, especially those who have problems with their lungs.
